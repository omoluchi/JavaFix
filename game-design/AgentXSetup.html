GAME SET UP

To play this game, you will need 4 players on a webcam call. NOTE: Having a screenshare tool may be useful when showing your generated story. However, it is not necessary.

Here are some common webcam applications:

  • Zoom
  • Google Teams
  • Skype
  • Discord

As long as you are on a group call with your team, the game is playable!
